Drama

This is the youngest art that is cultivated in Madlenianum. The first drama production, a piece of TESLA from Milos Crnjanski, however, it is confirmed that in this third type of theater fostered distinctive avant-garde artistic ideas Madlenianum. That is until today, and the latest drama production: ANA KARENJINA.
