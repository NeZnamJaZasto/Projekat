Its founder wish, Madeleine Zepter was to see on the stage of hes theater classic and contemporary work set in an innovative and more modern version. She wanted to bring the spirit of the world in Belgrade and Zemun.

To this date it has been set up 50 productions and 110 performances in the country and abroad. The play was seen by over 400,000 visitors. The first opera performed was a secret marriage Domenico Cimarosa.
