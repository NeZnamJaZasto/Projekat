Opera

From the opera "The Secret Marriage" by Domenico Cimarosa, which is open to the then Madlenianum Opera House, to the Ruder Leoncavallo, on the stage of Madlenianum each production shifted the standards in their field. Explore new opera term started with the chamber opera, to joining the completely renovated building Madlenianum opera outgrown their chamber frame and started to test new forms of performing and directing.
