The building which today houses MADLENIANUM began in the fifties, and put into operation the seventies of the last century, as the Zemun National Theatre. Original architectural and technical-technological concept of the building before the all-out reduktivnoscu his model-turned-social-realist culture than the modern home theater. The operational period of thirty years, during which not a lot of investments, particularly degrading period of the nineties (when the building becomes even mall), he did his own. When MADLENIANUM, and then a new institution, 1998. Moved into the theater building in Zemun, was found to have a completely run-down, dilapidated and derelict building.

Decision of its founder, Mrs. Zepter, launched the process of capital phase of reconstruction, whose main aim was to object not only bring in a function, but also assimilate the spirit of the time, new technical possibilities, and thus enable the achievement of the most complex artistic stage procedures, concepts and settings. Today we can say that these aspirations fully realized and MADLENIANUM is undoubtedly one of the most representative theater building, not only in Belgrade and Serbia, but in the wider region.

The new architectural design (the work of academician prof. Arch. Ivana Antica) modern, but at the same time carefully into the surroundings and context of old Zemun, outlined the exceptional importance of the public function of the theater, but they reaffirmed and potential places. This quality is especially noticeable on Decision small square in front of the building, which is sensibly treated as an area of overlapping internal and external, and appears as the expansion of the foyer, or, again, as a kind of urban stage.


The dominant theatrical feature technologically rounded redesign of the entrance: sale, spaces, artistic and technical ensemble and others. However, a wealth of new buildings MADLENIANUM in numerous, almost infinitely alternative possibilities of utilization.
