Musical

In the new decade Madlenianum found its place in the scene of musical. The first act of this kind, Les Miserables, immediately brought attention. This unique musica from Boublila and Schonberg is set in an unforgettable creation director Nebojsa Bradic and performed exceptional cast of actors and singers, led by the bard of our pop scene Zafira Hadzimanovim.

Encouraged by this success, we set in 2012 year new musical Rebecca, Sylvester by Levaia and Michael Kuncea in another successful directed by Nebojsa Bradic.
